How to update scores

Who can update scores?

Anyone who has been given Editor access to the Google Sheet. The sheet owner (Matias) can share it with specific people using their email address.

How to enter a score

  1. Open the WC2026 Scores spreadsheet
  2. Find the match row — e.g. G_1 = Group G, 2nd match (Iran vs New Zealand)
  3. Enter the home score in column B and away score in column C
  4. The tracker updates automatically within 60 seconds

Match ID reference

Format is GroupLetter_MatchNumber where match number starts at 0.
Example: G_0 = Belgium vs Egypt  |  G_1 = Iran vs New Zealand  |  G_3 = New Zealand vs Egypt

Auto-refresh

This page refreshes scores every 60 seconds automatically. You can also click the Refresh scores button on the Groups tab anytime.
